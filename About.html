ABOUT KBS

Spices have a long and ancient history, especially in india where they are a part of life and heritage. In every household and every provience across the country, different spices and blends are used to create distinct and distinct flavours in cuisine. Many dacades ago, housewives used to grind their spices manually at home and make their own mixture for the use in their cooking. To make this process easier for the housewife 'KAMAL BHAGWATI SHYAM' looked at the concept of ready-to-use ground spices.

Today, spices worth crores of rupees are manufactured and packaged by modern machines and sold through network of stockiest and millions of retail dealers across India. KBS MASALA UDHYOG established in Churu, Rajasthan. Rajasthan is one of India's Popular spice manufactures in powdered from including seasoning products under the brand name of KBS Masale.KBS Masale is India's largest spice processing startup Udhyog that has all its processing facilities in-house.
